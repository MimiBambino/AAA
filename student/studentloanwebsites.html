Reputable Nonprofit Websites

Not all nonprofit organizations are equal. The following are the best of the best student loan nonprofit websites (note the lack of third party advertising).

  • Student Loan Borrower Assistance by the National Consumer Law Center (NCLC). NCLC is the premier consumer rights resource for consumers and attorneys alike.
  • Project on Student Debt is run by TICAS (The Institute for College Access and Success) a leading advocate for students' right to affordable education. It has many resources for student loan borrowers.
  • U.S. PIRG is a consumer rights advocacy group with a strong interest in keeping higher education affordable.
Useful Government Websites
  • The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau has many resources for student loan borrowers. As a new government agency it has a particularly sleek and useful website.
  • The Department of Education has a good website. You can apply for any kind of federal student loan program through this website for free.
  • StudentLoans.gov is another Department of Education resource with direct links to application forms. You will need your FSA ID and password to log in. You can also obtain your FSA ID and password through this website.
